World of Warcraft is a Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game (MMORPG) set in the world of Azeroth, a world strife with confict as the honorable Alliance and vigilant Horde battle it out over power and resources.

Players can play as one of nineteen races, each with their own backstory and racial abilities, and twelve classes, each with multiple specializations that change the way you play. Battle against powerful dungeon bosses with groups of five, or fight against dangerous raid bosses in groups of up to 30 people, or grind it out face to face in player versus player combat.

The game is currently in its seventh expansion with the release of Battle for Azeroth, which introduced two new continents, Zulduzar and Kul Tiras, warfronts, a 20 player PvE mode against enemy faction NPCs, and four new allied races for both the Horde and the Alliance.
